Commands

Daily Claim

__Every day you can do the following:__

!daily - Claim an accumulating reward that increases in value for every consecutive day logged in and resets your instance dungeons keys. After 4 weeks (28 days of consecutive logins) the rewards restart anew.

!dailydraw - Draw 10x Cards

(Both commands reset at 10:00 PM UTC)

Gambling

__The heart and joy of nattobot:__

There are 5 different Gambling commands for Nattobot:

.coinflip / .cf

- Throws a coin for Heads or Tails against the bot with a max. bet amount of 5000 Natto Coins.

.rps

- Play Rock, Paper, Scissors against the bot with a max. bet amount of 5000 Natto Coins. Optionally, you can add your choice to the command like rock, paper, scissors or the integer equivalent of them being 0, 1, 2. If you donâ€™t, it will be a random choice.

Example:

.rps 5000 1 / .rps 5000 paper
